About me

Learning and expanding my skillset has always been a passion of mine. Back in 2020, while studying engineering, I explored different industries to figure out what I truly wanted to do. That’s when I had the chance to work as a UX/UI designer at a startup, and I quickly realised how much I enjoyed solving problems by understanding users. Even without a formal design background at the time, my curiosity pushed me to learn everything I could—keeping up with new technologies and focusing on user-centric solutions.


Today, I’ve completed my master’s in interaction design, gaining hands-on experience in user research, problem-solving, and working with people from diverse backgrounds. My engineering background is a valuable asset in my design work. I understand technical requirements and translate concepts into practical, development-ready solutions, ensuring a seamless transition from design to implementation.


In my free time, I love cooking—experimenting with new recipes and exploring cuisines from around the world. I’m also passionate about filmmaking, using it as a creative way to express my ideas and perspectives.
